# Programmed Death 1 (PD-1)

**Biology**
- Expressed on T-cells

**Function**
- Co-receptor of [[protein_pdl1|PD-L1]] and [[protein_pdl2|PD-L2]]
- Mediator of immune tolerance

**Disease Relevance**
- Leveraged by cancer cells to evade T-cell-mediated immunity
