#ref #cell-culture #recipe

_T-cell media supports the growth of primary T-cells isolated from murine or human sources._

# Murine T-cell media
---
JK T-cell media
- RPMI
	- 10% FBS
	- 1% Pen-Strep
	- 50uL Beta-mercaptoethanol
	- IL-2 100 U/mL

# Human T-cell media
---
DT T-cell media
- RPMI
	- 10% FBS, **heat-inactivated**
	- 1% Pen-Strep
	- IL-2 100 U/mL (double-check?)
	- **Sterile-filtered**
