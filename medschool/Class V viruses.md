# Class V Viruses

## Characteristics
- Use (-) strand RNA as genomic material
- **Carry their own [[RNA-dependent RNA polymerase]]**
- Replicate in cytosol **except** influenza (cannibalizes 5' caps)
## Major Class V Viruses
- [[hantavirus]]
- [[mumps]]
- [[measles]]
- [[RSV]]
- [[influenza]]
- [[rabies]]
- [[ebola]]
