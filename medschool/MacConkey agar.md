# MacConkey Agar

- **Selective**: No
- **Differential**: Yes

## Diagnostics
- MacConkey Agar is infused with a pH-sensitive dye and lactose
- Certain bacteria are **lactose-fermenters**
	- Lactose fermentation causes drop in pH
	- Bacteria take up red dye
