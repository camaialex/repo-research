# Cephalosporins

## Classification
- [[beta-lactam]]
## Pharmacodynamics
- Blocks Stage 3 [[bacterial cell wall]] synthesis by inhibiting PBP2a
- Bactericidal
- High selective toxicity
## Pharmacokinetics
- [[oral]] administration
- Renal clearance
## Resistance Mechanisms
- Extended-spectrum beta-lactamase
- Alteration of PBP2a (MRSA)

- First generation
