# Primitive Streak

The primitive streak is an _outgrowth_ of cells from the primordial [[epiblast]]. It gives rise to the [[mesoderm]] and [[endoderm]].

Critical supplier of biomass to the rest of the body in early development.

### Mnemonics
- "Tissue factory"
