# Arterioles
## Function
- Carries blood _away from_ the body
## Structure
- [[tunica intima]]: **No** internal elastica
- [[tunica media]]: Thinner than that of arteries
- [[tunica adventitia]]: Thin, blends in
## Histology
- Lumen is round and patent
