# Penicillins

## Classification
- [[beta-lactam]]
## Pharmacodynamics
- Inhibits [[bacterial cell wall]] synthesis via inhibition of PBP2a
- [[bactericidal]]
- High selective toxicity
## Pharmacokinetics
- [[oral]] administration
- Renal clearance
## Resistance Mechanisms
- **Narrow-spectrum beta-lactamase** (MSSA)
- Alteration of PBP2a (MRSA)
## Examples
- Penicillin V
- Amoxicillin
