# Gangrenous Necrosis

Special subtype of [[coagulative necrosis]] ("dry") or [[liquefactive necrosis]] ("wet")
## Etiology
- Accumulation of **iron deposits** (black) in tissue due to lysis of [[erythrocyte]]s
