# Galpha-i

Inhibitory counterpart of Gs signaling
## Process
1. Ligand binds to GPCR
2. Gi protein binds GTP and dissociates
3. Gi protein inhibits [[adenylate cyclase]]
4. [[cAMP]] levels stay low
5. Signaling pathway is not engaged
