# Pattern Recognition Receptor

- **Innate** immune receptor that responds to specific type of conserved antigen
	- [[PAMP]]s - pathogens
	- [[DAMP]]s - sterile injury
- Once activated, PRRs trigger the formation of [[inflammasome]]s
