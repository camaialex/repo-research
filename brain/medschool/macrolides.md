## Pharmacodynamics

- Bacteriostatic
## Pharmacokinetics
- [[oral]] administration
- Accumulates in [[lungs]]
- **Non-renal clearance**
## Resistance Mechanisms
- Efflux transporter
- Alteration of 50S ribosome
