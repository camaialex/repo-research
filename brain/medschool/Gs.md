# Galpha-s

## Process
1. Ligand binds GPCR
2. Gs binds GTP and dissociates from membrane
3. Gs activates [[adenylate cyclase]]
4. Adenylate cyclase produces [[cAMP]] from [[ATP]]
5. High cAMP levels stimulate [[PKA]]
6. PKA triggers a signaling cascade
